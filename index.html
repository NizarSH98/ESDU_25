25 Years Advancing Sustainable Livelihoods

ESDU champions community-led development, resilient food systems, and inclusive growth— bridging academia and the field across Lebanon and the region.

Explore Impact See the Journey

Mission, Vision, Core Values

Grounded in research. Powered by communities. Focused on lasting change.

Mission

Harness education, research, and development to advance sustainable and resilient food systems, serving and empowering marginalized and rural communities through research, training, and outreach.

Vision

A world where communities and ecosystems thrive together — a network of self‑generative eco‑communities built on climate‑smart agriculture, circular economy, and local food systems.

Core Values

  • Local Roots, Global Wings
  • Innovation under Uncertainty

ESDU at Work

What is ESDU? Explore how research translates to real-world impact.

Education & Training

Capacity building, CEC courses, school programs, and online learning (KariaNet) that grow skills and leadership.

R&D Projects

Applied research linking field insights to development: circular economy, AI/digital, climate‑smart design.

Community Outreach

People‑centered approaches supporting farmers, women, and youth to build resilient livelihoods.

Communication & Dissemination

Knowledge sharing and visibility through publications, events, and regional networks.

Strategic Partnerships

Institutional and grassroots coalitions for transformative change and policy engagement.

Multistakeholder Initiatives

Keepers of the Land, Urban Oasis, and living labs bridging academia and practice.

Keepers of the Land

ESDU’s flagship initiative connecting small producers to markets, celebrating local foods, and sustaining rural livelihoods.
